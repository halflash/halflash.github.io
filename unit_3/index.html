About

We are a team formed to improve financial literacy amongst Singapore Malay/Muslim Community. We love our religion, culture and tradition but we love our money too. We hope to engage in discussions on financial challenges the community face when making life decisions such as:

  • Getting Married,
  • Housing,
  • Starting a Family
  • and Retirement
